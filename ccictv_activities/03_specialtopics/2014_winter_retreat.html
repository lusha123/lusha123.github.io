呂紹昌牧師

呂牧師曾任亞特蘭大教會主任牧師，正道福音神學院教務長與代理院長，目前擔任基督工人神學院院長。呂牧師獲三一神學院道學碩士與哲學博士， 對教會牧養、培育領袖、文字事工等多有負擔，著有《天道以賽亞書註釋卷一》，《說地談天：論時事想天國》，以及其他學術著作，並在《傳揚》雜誌上撰寫每月信仰專欄。

 

Dr. Steve Diehl

Steve has ministered to youth and adults for over 39 years, including serving as pastor for twenty two of those years. Steve is the founder of Forgiveness Min-istries, travels extensively giving seminars on forgiveness and is the author of Developing a Lifestyle of Forgiveness. He is also the radio teacher and host of "Focus on Forgiveness." He and his wife, Becky, have been married since 1980 and have six children.
